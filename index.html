What is TourDePark?

Simple... it's a beer crawl. Have one (or a few) beer per park and enjoy Toronto's greenery. Oh, and we travel park to park by bycicle. For the day, we are a bike gang. Bike gang uniformas may one day be incorporated. For now, just wear what makes you comfortable.
